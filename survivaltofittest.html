Sarjan Setu

Welcome to the structural engineering challenge! Participants will demonstrate their skills by building structures to stand againts weight and earthquakes using candy sticks and Fevicol. With materials provided, apply your knowledge effectively to create durable designs. Get ready to showcase your ingenuity in this exciting event!

Event Description

• Group Event (3 Persons)
• This event focuses on how participants make structures to with stand weight and earthquakes and are durable
• Apply your physical laws and empirical knowledge of structural engineering in an effective and efficient way to build a structure using candy sticks and fevicol.
• The materials required for building the bridge candy sticks, fevicol, will be provided to the teams.

Event Rules

• The time duration given would be 2 hours.
• Structure dimension span 30 cm (minimum) and width 8 to 10 cm.
• The use of thread is not allowed. Only fevicol can be used as an adhesive.
• Participants must bring basic stationary items such as pens, scissors, cutters.
• Weight ranging up to 3 kg would be placed on the top surface of the structure.
• The maximum weight the bridge can hold without collapsing would be noted.
