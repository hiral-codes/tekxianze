About the Event

TEKXIANZE 2024 is the Annual Technical Festival of S. S. Agrawal Institute of Engineering & Technology, Navsari where megaminds from engineering colleges all over the state come together and lock horns to find the ultimate geeks. It is loaded with events for students with all kinds of interests and skills like coding, wiring,and entertainment.
TEKXIANZE#2K24 is loaded with venues and platforms for people with all sorts of skills to showcase and prove their mettle.Fasten your seatbelts and get ready to be mind-blown in the company of tech-sharks. If perspective is what you need, this is where you should be!
TEKXIANZE#2K24 aims to be the first fully green and eco-friendly college fest in the state, with its motto and theme - GO GREEN!
