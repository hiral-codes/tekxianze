Mech Quiz

Event Rules

• Group Event (Maximum 2 persons)
ROUND 1: Elimination Round (Rules for Elimination Round)
• This round has 10 questions from various mechanical domains.
• Each team will be asked questions paper of 1 mark each. They will be given 15 minutes for 10 questions.
• If the allotted team is unable to answer the question, then team will be eliminated.
• Only Qualified Teams would be selected for FINAL ROUND.
• Judge’s decision will be final.

ROUND 2: Final Round (Rules for Final Round)
• There will be 10 questions in this round. Team Raise the hand first will be given the chance to answer.
• 2 marks will be awarded for each correct answer & 1 mark will be deducted for each incorrect answer.
• Team will be given 30 seconds for each question.
• In case of tie between 2 or more teams, further 3 questions would be asked for final selection.
• At the end of the final round highest marks team will win and second highest marks team will be runner up.
