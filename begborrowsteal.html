Beg borrow steal

Get ready to put all your resources and contacts to a test to fetch the most radical and extraordinary things in this epic race. In this game you can win the competition by begging, borrowing or stealing things.

Participants will be given a list of things that they have to fetch within a time span. The team which finds the most items on the list within a time span wins! So get ready to have some real fun.

Event Rules

• All teams will be provided with a list of items.
• Team members will have to complete the task within a time limit of 15 minutes (try to use the minimum time possible).
• Team members will have to bring at least 7 things to qualify for winning list. (try to bring the maximum items from the list.)
• The team can either beg, borrow or steal the items.
• Teams will be judged on the basis of a number of items fetched within the time limit.
• For final judgement the minimum time utilized and the maximum items grabbed will be considered.
• Participants are requested to avoid wearing valuable items like wristwatches and jewellery during the event.
• Participants are requested to wear sports shoes.

Kindly make sure to return the items to its original owner once your turn is over.
