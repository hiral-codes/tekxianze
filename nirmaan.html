Nirmaan

This event focuses on how participants make structures engineered to withstand weight and earthquakes, and are durable. Structural Engineers are trained to design the “Bones and Muscles” of man-made structures. Come take part and apply your physical laws, and empirical knowledge of structural engineering in an effective and efficient way to build a structure using broom sticks and thread.

Event Description

• The time duration given would be 90 mins.
• The materials required for building the structure will be provided to the teams.
• The complete structure would be first measured by the coordinators.
• If the dimensions are found to exceed the allowed dimensions by a considerate margin, the team will be disqualified.

Material to be used

• The organizers will provide limited broom sticks and thread.
• Sticks can be altered physically by cutting or notching at any angle.
• Only thread can be used as to join the member; using other material may lead to disqualification.
• Participants must bring basic stationary items, such as pens, scissors, cutters, etc.
• thread would be provided to the participants.

Structure Dimensions

• Length: 15 cm (minimum) - 20 cm (maximum)
• Width: 15 cm (minimum) - 20 cm (maximum)
• Height: 30 cm (minimum) (No limit for maximum height)

Team Specifications

• The team should have a minimum of 3 members.
• Members of a team may be from the same college or different.
• Any number of teams can participate from one college.
• Only students can participate. Professionals are not allowed.
• Each team must specify their Team Leader at registration time.
• All the important communications between the organizer and the registered team will be done through their Team Leader. The Team Leader must submit valid contact detail (mobile no) at the time of registration.

Event Rules

• Bring your college/student I-Card at the time of the competition
• Decisions taken by Judges will be final and binding for all.
• Any misbehaviour will lead you to disqualification from the competition
• Organizers have the right to reject entries for any inappropriate team behaviour.
• Teams who don’t show up on allotted slots will be disqualified.
• If less than 30 teams have registered for the event only two prizes will be given and if less than 15 teams have registered for the event only one prize will be given.
