CHEM-O-RAINBOW

CHEM-O-RAINBOW is a “Different colour solution Making Competition” to showcase the innovation, technical skill and creativity of students in field of Chemical engineering. In this event, team of students will be provided by required material (Chemical compound) and they have to prepare different coloured solutions from allocated materials.

Event Rules

Team event (In a team of two members)

Screening Round

• In case of more than 25 teams registered, there will be screen round of multiple choice Q & A.
• Each team will be provided with a set of question paper containing 25 MCQ questions. (Related to General knowledge, awareness, basic technical knowledge).
• Time limit: 20 minutes
• Only 12 teams will be selected based on their marks in MCQ test.
• Selected team will enter in the final round.

Final Round

• The selected teams, in this round, will be given a Chemical compound.
• 30 minutes will be given to each team for making of coloured solution

General Rules

• Registration is free
• Only team can participate in this event.
• Certificates will be given to the registered participants after successful participation in the event.
• Winners will be awarded with prize and certificate.
• Registered participant have to come on time according to pre-decided time schedule as informed.
• The students who fail to remain present on the time of event will be disqualified without any notification.
• During the event, if anyone found guilty in the group the entire group will be disqualify and also a strict action will be taken.
• The decision of judge is final and abiding; no complaints regarding the judgment will be entertained.
• Judgement will be taken on the basis of maximum no of coloured solution prepare.
