Event Rules

1. Student must have to participant in the group of 3 students only.
2. Participants have to reach the destination by reflecting laser through mirrors covering maximum distance and points in minimum time through all the mandatory checkpoints.
3. 1 Inch = 1 Point
4. Time Limit = 60 Seconds
5. Compulsory four mirrors have to be used.
6. Rules for the next round will be declared on the spot during events.
7. Rules can be modified by the faculty event coordinator according to the condition.
8. During the registration and event day all participants have to wear College I-Card compulsory.
9. Participants who have registered themselves in this particular event have to look for the notice board regarding any updates of particular events.
10. Registered participant have to come on time according to pre-decided time schedule as displayed at notice board.
11. The students who fail to remain present on the time of event will be disqualified without any notification.
12. During the event, if anyone found guilty in the group the entire group will be disqualify and also a strict action will be taken.
13. All participants must have to co-operate the student coordinator.
14. Decision taken by faculty coordinator of this particular event will remain final.
15. NO COMPLAINTS REGARDING THE JUDGEMENT WILL BE ENTERTAINED.
