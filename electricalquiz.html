Electrical quiz

Event Description

• It’s a team event having 2 members per team.
• For this particular event, basic knowledge of mathematics and electrical engineering will be essential Such as, Elements of Electrical Engineering, Basic Electronics, All basic and Fundamental rules, maths concept, and general knowledge of particular field.

Round: 1

This is the word search/word find/word seek round in which participants have to search a word from the letters of words placed in a grid form. Participants have to search a 24 word within 15 minutes. In which 1 mark for right answer. The objective of this round is to find and mark all the words hidden inside the box. The words may be placed horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

Round: 2

This is the electrical quiz round. Participants have to solve 30 MCQ based questions in 30 minutes.

Round: 3 (Tie breaker round if necessary)

In the event of a tie at the end of the regular rounds of the quiz competition, a tiebreaker round will be conducted to determine the winner. The tiebreaker round will consist of a series of rapid-fire questions posed to the tied contestants. Each contestant will have a limited amount of time to answer each question, typically around 10-15 seconds. The questions in the tiebreaker round will cover a range of topics and difficulty levels, similar to those in the regular rounds. The contestant who correctly answers the most questions in the tiebreaker round will be declared the winner.

If the tie persists after the tiebreaker round, additional tiebreaker rounds may be conducted until a clear winner emerges. In the event of technical difficulties or other unforeseen circumstances preventing the tiebreaker round from proceeding as planned, the quizmaster or organizing committee may implement alternative methods for breaking the tie.

All decisions regarding the conduct and outcome of the tiebreaker round will be made by the quizmaster or organizing committee and are final."
