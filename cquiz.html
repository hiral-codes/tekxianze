C - Quiz

C Quiz is for all those students, who love to do programming and want to become programmer. Score more and you will be the winner.

Competition Structure

• Individual
• Individual participant who score more will be the winner.
• There will be 2 rounds. Cumulative score will be considered for final result.
• Charges for the participation is Rs. 50 per participant.

Event Rules

• There will be two rounds.
Victory Condition: Participant score maximum marks. (Total = Round1 score+Round2 score)
Round 1: This round will consist of 30 multiple questions each carrying 1 Marks. (No Negative Marking)
Round 2: Shortlisted participants from round 1 have to solve one program(solve error in the program) and do coding of small program related to C. Each question is of 10 marks.

Basic Details

• Registration is mandatory for all students.
• Quiz will be conducted in 2 rounds.
• Quiz will have 30 questions each carrying 1 mark.
• Attempting all questions is compulsory.
• Both the rounds have time limitations of 30 minutes.
• To qualify for Round 2 each student must score at least 50 % marks.
• Multiple answers will not be accepted.
• Consists of MCQS with basic C questions, programs and True/False.
• Coding will be conducted in college lab itself.
• Cheating should be avoided or else you will be disqualified.
• Any decision by the organizing committee is final.

A participant may be cautioned and be sent off if he/she commits any of the following offences:

• Receives more than one warning.
• Is guilty of violent conduct.
• Uses any unlawful or unfair proceedings.
• Is not present at event location at prescribed schedule.
• Participant must respect the spirit of sincerity.
