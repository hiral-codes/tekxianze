Counter Strike

This event is for all those gamers out there looking for an opportunity to show-off their unusual talent, hold your horses because here comes LAN Gaming competitive event with counter strike game on the table. Defeat others and you’re the Gaming King.

Competition Structure

Competition Method:

•5 vs. 5 (Team Play, 5 players per team)
• The competition will be knock-out. The team who wins their 1st round advances to the next stage.
• The knock out continues till we get final 2 teams.
• There will not be any match played for the third spot.
• Charges for the participation are Rs. 250 per team. (50 per participant)

Rules

1. 5 players per team.
2. Victory Condition: Team winning max rounds. (ex. 5 rounds total, 3 for win)
3. Official Maps: de_dust2, de_inferno, de_nuke, de_train
4. (a) Toss will decide which team will be CT and T.
(b) The Server Master will record the demo of the match.
5. Round Time: 2 minute.
6. C4 Timer: 35 seconds.
7. Players are expected to bring their gaming accessories. (Headphones)
8. Basic configuration files (CFG) will be allowed with no illegal scripts.
9. Any decision by the organizing committee is final.
The only exception is when all team members are eliminated in the game. .

A player may be cautioned and be sent off if he commits any of the following offences: -
1. Receives more than one warning.
2. Is guilty of violent conduct.
3. Uses any unlawful or unfair proceedings.
4. Misleads or dupes any tournament official.
5. Is not present at his match schedule.
6. Player must respect the spirit of fair play and non-violence.
Additional softwares like mouse_fix and dll’s will not be allowed.
